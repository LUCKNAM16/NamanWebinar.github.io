SERENE BEACHES

The wide shore is a major attraction of this beach. The clear water, the silver sand and the golden sun gifts the beach with a majestic beauty. The wide shoreline and the gradual inclination into the water make it a safe beach. One can have a variety of activities at this beach.

  1. RADHANAGAR BEACH, ANDAMAN AND NICOBAR ISLANDS

    This is the second-longest beach in Asia. Radhanagar Beach is the star attraction of Havelock Island and rightly so. A vast stretch of glittering white sand beach flanked by lush forests of tropical trees, it is a piece of heaven on earth.

    BEST TIME TO VISIT-OCTOBER TO MAY

  2. MARARI BEACH, KERALA

    Marari Beach is a beach at Mararikulam village in Alappuzha District of Kerala, India, 11 km from Alappuzha town. Mararikulam is connected by rail and has a railway station by the same name. It is also well connected by road. NH 66 passes through S.L.Puram, which is 5 km to the east of Mararikulam. Nearest airport is Cochin International Airport. It was rated as one of the worlds top five HAMMOCK BEACH by National Geographic survey.

    BEST TIME TO VISIT- DECEMBER TO MARCH

  3. TALASARI BEACH, ODISHA


    Talasari Beach is a beach in the Baleswar district of Odisha, India. It lies on the north-eastern coast of India. Talasari Beach is a beach in the Baleswar district of Odisha, India. It lies on the north-eastern coast of India. The name Talasari is derived from the two words Tala and Sari/Sarani.

  4. BHOGWE BEACH, KUDAL


    Bhogwe Beach is amongst the loveliest and most secluded beaches in Konkan. Bhogwe Beach is in Bhogwe village near Nivati Fort. At the Bhogwe Village, there is the amalgamation of the Karli River and the Arabian Sea. Located on the Sagari Mahamarg joining Malvan to Vengurla Bhogwe. Bhogwe Beach is endowed with bubbly waves and shiny sand. Bhogwe Beach is popular for sublime beach and therefore a popular gateway.

    BEST TIME TO VISIT- OCTOBER TO FEBRARY
